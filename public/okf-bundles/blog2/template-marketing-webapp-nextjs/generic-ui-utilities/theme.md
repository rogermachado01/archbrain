It is used on the root route (/) and on dynamic slug-based pages (/[slug]), meaning it applies consistently whether a visitor lands on the homepage or navigates to any content page resolved by slug. Its placement across both route types indicates it functions as a shared layout-level or wrapper concern rather than something tied to a single page's unique content.

As part of the generic-ui-utilities grouping, Theme sits alongside other cross-cutting UI helpers, reflecting its role as foundational infrastructure that supports rendering across the app rather than a one-off feature component.
